About

Hackfinity Hackathon is a unique event that brings together students, young professionals, and other individuals around the world.

?

What!

Hackfinity brings innovators together to develop tech-driven solutions for real-world challenges.

Why!

Participants will form teams, ideate, prototype, and present solutions over 24 hours — guided by mentors and powered by cutting-edge tools.

Restrictions & Regulations

Please read all the restrictions and regulations carefully to ensure a smooth and fair participation experience.

Regulations

  • The Problem statement only for the IoT will be provided two or three days before 9th of the May, for other domains on spot Problem statement will be given.
  • Participants should bring their own Laptops, Chargers, IOT devices, any additional hardware needed.
  • Participants need to bring their College ID cards.
  • The hackathon will be conducted in offline mode.
  • Team Leaders will receive Registration Confirmation Mail upon entry finalization.
  • Registration fee is Rs.1000 per Team (non-refundable).
  • Team must have between 2 to 4 members.

Restrictions

  • Participants must follow instructions from the organizing committee.
  • Teams must register before the deadline. Late entries will not be accepted.
  • Internet usage is allowed for research, APIs, and documentation.
  • Decisions made by the judging panel will be final and binding.
  • Judging will be based on innovation, technical complexity, UI/UX, and presentation.
  • Top teams will be awarded exciting prizes and certificates. (Domain Specific Prizes)
